For sake of comparison number 1 is Denmark, Belgium is 28 and Poland 33. Anyway It is cool that many public services have been made available online. I just wonder what the ranking would be if they considered security. It is relatively simple to push public services online, but if these are not properly secured this can create more serious problems than the ones it solves. ...
